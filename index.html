Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients :

Penne Pasta (200g or approx. 2 cups), Heavy Cream (1 cup), Butter (2 tbsp), Parmesan Cheese (1/2 cup, grated), Garlic (2 cloves, minced), Salt, Black Pepper, Fresh Parsley (for garnish).

Serving Size :

2

Recipe

  1. Boil the penne pasta in salted water until al dente, reserving a splash of pasta water before draining.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet and sauté the minced garlic for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer gently for 3-4 minutes to reduce and thicken the sauce.
  4. Reduce heat to low and whisk in the parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until the sauce is smooth.
  5. Toss the pasta into the sauce until fully coated, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.

Arrabbiata Pasta

Ingredients :

Penne Pasta (200g), Canned Crushed Tomatoes (400g), Garlic (2 cloves), Dried Red Chili Flakes (1 tsp), Olive Oil, Fresh Parsley, Salt.

Serving Size :

2

Recipe

  1. Boil the penne pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain well.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the minced garlic and red chili flakes for 1 minute.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pan and simmer on low heat for 10 minutes to thicken.
  4. Season the sauce with salt, then toss the cooked pasta into the pan until evenly coated.
  5. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and serve hot.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients :

Fusilli or Spaghetti (200g), Fresh Basil Leaves (2 cups), Pine Nuts or Walnuts (1/4 cup), Garlic (2 cloves), Parmesan Cheese (1/2 cup), Olive Oil (1/2 cup).

Serving Size :

2

Recipe

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente, reserving a small cup of water before draining.
  2. Blend basil, nuts, garlic, parmesan, and olive oil together until smooth to make the pesto.
  3. Place the hot drained pasta back in the pot (heat off) and pour the fresh pesto over it.
  4. Toss well to coat, adding a splash of reserved pasta water if the sauce feels too thick.
  5. Serve immediately with a little extra parmesan cheese sprinkled on top.
